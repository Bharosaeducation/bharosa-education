About Us

Bharosa Education is a learning platform dedicated to helping students improve their knowledge and skills. We provide quality education, guidance, and support for academic success.

Bharosa Education Consultancy is a trusted educational consultancy based in Itahari, Nepal. We are dedicated to helping students achieve their dreams of studying abroad through professional counselling and personalized guidance.

Our team assists students in selecting the right course, university and country according to their academic qualifications, interests and career goals.

We provide complete support throughout the admission process, including university applications, documentation, visa guidance and pre-departure preparation.

"तपाईंको सपना, हाम्रो जिम्मेवारी।" We are committed to helping students build a successful future through quality international education.
